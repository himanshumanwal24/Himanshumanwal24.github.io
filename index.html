About Me

The person behind the process

I'm a Technical Support Leader who has spent over nine years in SaaS and cloud-based environments. I don't just handle escalations — I build the systems that prevent them from recurring.

My work sits at the intersection of deep technical troubleshooting and strategic operations: diagnosing production failures, driving root-cause fixes with engineering, and building the documentation and automation that makes entire teams faster.

Currently acting as Technical Support Manager at Lonza, I lead L3 escalation ownership, mentor support engineers, and have introduced AI-assisted workflows that measurably improved team efficiency. Before that, I built a strong foundation across eClinicalWorks and Maruti Techlabs — handling everything from multi-tenant SaaS issues to authentication, API integrations, and system reliability.

I care about one thing above all: making support a function that compounds in value over time, not just a cost center that reacts.
